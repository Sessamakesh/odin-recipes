Rice Pilaf

This is a fantastically simple side dish that goes with just about anything. The flavors of mirepoix help to support chicken, beef, turkey, pork, any meat you can think of. That makes this dish a versatile and well rounded option that can work with any main dish you desire.

Ingredients

  • 1 large white onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 2 cups wild rice
  • 2 tbs butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash the celery and peel the onion and carrot
  2. Finely dice the vegetables
  3. In a medium sized pot add the wild rice and butter and cook until browned, stirring constantly
  4. Next add 2 cups of water and bring to a boil
  5. Once the water is boiling cover and turn heat to low
  6. While the rice is cooking add the veggies to a sauté pan with some butter and sauté until tender
  7. Once the rice has absorbed the water add it to the veggies and mix thoroughly
  8. Salt and pepper to taste!
