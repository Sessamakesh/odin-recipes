Mashed Potatoes

Mashed potatoes. What more does one need to say? You take potatoes,you mash them, add some flavor, and boom you have a rockin' side dish that people are sure to ask for more of.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of potatoes
  • 2 large onions
  • Garlic
  • Sour Cream
  • Fresh herbs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. This step is optional Peel potatoes
  2. Cut potatoes into cubes of roughly the same size
  3. Dice Onions
  4. Add onions, garlic, and potatoes to large pot and cover with water
  5. Bring to a boil and cook until you can insert a fork into the potatoes with ease
  6. Strain the water from the potatoes and transfer to a bowl
  7. MASH!
  8. Add a stick of butter and sour cream, salt, and pepper to taste
  9. Garnish with fresh herbs
