Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 large white onion
  • 1 large carrot
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 1 box preferred noodles
  • 2 tbs Butter
  • 2 tbs Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In an oven safe dish place the two chicken breasts and 2 cups of chicken stock
  3. Place in the oven and cook for about 30 minutes or until the internal temp is 165
  4. While the chicken cooks rinse the celery and peel the carrot and onion
  5. Dice the vegetables into pieces of your preferred sizeI personally prefer to dice my vegetables very finely so that they cook a little faster and mix better but any size or shape will work
  6. Add your vegetables to a pot with the butter and garlic and sautee until the garlic becomes aromatic
  7. Once the chicken is done shred or dice and add to vegetables
  8. Next add the remaining 4 cups of stock and water if necessary to cover the ingredients
  9. Bring the stock to a boil and add your preferred noodlesI like to use egg noodles
  10. Once noodles are cooked to your liking turn the heat down to keep the pot simmering and serve hot
