Granola

Granola is a simple snack thats easy to make and delicious for everyone. This recipe is one of my favorites. I actually make it for the restaurant that I currently work at. I could write more about the history of granola but personally I hate long introductions to recipes so lets get in to it.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Oats
  • 1/2 cup Walnuts
  • 1/2 cup Pecans
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup Canola Oil
  • 1/2 cup Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees
  2. Roughly chop walnuts and pecans
  3. Mix all ingredients
  4. Put some parchment paper down on a sheet tray
  5. Either form your mixture into bars or just evenly coat the tray
  6. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes

    7. The granola will still be a little soft when it comes out of the oven but will harden as it cools

  7. Remove from oven and allow to cool
  8. If making bars bag them and store for later. If making loose granola break up the tray into smaller pieces and store
